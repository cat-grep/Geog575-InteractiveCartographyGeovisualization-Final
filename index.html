Fraud today is no longer a distant threat; it is something that quietly enters our inboxes, phones, and daily routines. Across Canada, people of all ages, income levels, and backgrounds are encountering scams that are more sophisticated, more convincing, and more costly than ever before.

Many of us recognize the uneasy feeling when receiving a suspicious text, an unexpected call, or a message that looks almost legitimate. That uncertainty is now a shared national experience.

This interactive dashboard helps you see fraud not as an abstract statistic, but as a risk that touches people just like you.
